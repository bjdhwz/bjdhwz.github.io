Random art generator that is based on a very simple rule: it picks a value, then randomly chooses whether to repeat that value or pick a new random one. Then it chooses whether to repeat these two or pick entirely new ones. Then it repeats the same with four, eight, sixteen and so on. The end result is a pattern that is not entirely random, but looks as if it's made out of a limited set of elements. This 'semi-randomness' is characteristic for pretty much any human technology, from circuit boards to urban grids.
